Privacy Policy for Bilingual Reader

Last Updated: 2025-09-13

Introduction

Welcome to Bilingual Reader. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our extension.

Information We Collect

When you activate the Bilingual Reader extension on a web page, we collect the following information:

  • The text content of the web page you are viewing.

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or browsing history.

How We Use Your Information

The text content we collect is used for the sole purpose of providing the translation service. We send the text to an external translation service to be translated, and then display the translated text back to you.

Information Sharing

To provide the translation, we share the text content of the web page with Google Translate (translate.googleapis.com). We do not share any other information with third parties.

Data Security

We do not store any of the text content that is collected. The information is transmitted to the translation service and used immediately to display the translation. Once the translation is complete, the information is discarded.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page.

Contact Us
